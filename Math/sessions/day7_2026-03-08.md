# Day 7 — Sunday 8 March

**Focus:** Methods drill — naming and practising the 4 superpowers
**Duration:** ~15 minutes
**Format:** Introduction of named methods + practice questions

## Session Summary

Introduced the concept of "named methods" — framing them as superpowers Belle already uses but didn't have names for. Each method introduced one at a time with a practice question.

## Results

### PM = Plus
- **Question:** What's 2:30 pm in 24-hour time?
- **Answer:** 2:30 > 2+12=14 > 14:30 ✓
- Showed working unprompted. Solid.

### Bunny Hops
- **Question:** How long from 6:45 to 8:20?
- **Answer:** 6:45>[+15m]>7:00>[+1h]>8:00>[+20m] = 1h 35m ✓
- All hops written down. Added up correctly.

### Double Up
- **Question:** Cupcakes cost R150 each. You have R900. How many?
- **Answer:** 1x150=150, 2x150=300, 4x150=600, 6x150=900 → 6 ✓
- Clean doubling strategy. Every step shown.

### Round & Fix
- **Question:** 325 + 197 = ?
- **First attempt:** Rounded correctly (197→200, added 3) but subtracted 2 instead of 3 in the fix step. Got 523.
- **Self-correction:** When asked "how much did you add?", immediately said 3. Then did 525-3 but initially said 523, then corrected to 522 on second try. ✓
- Method understood. Minor arithmetic slip caught with prompting.

### Method Selection
- **Question:** "How long from 11:45 am to 2:10 pm?" — which method?
- **First answer:** PM = Plus (partial credit — would need PM=Plus for conversion, but the main method is Bunny Hops)
- **Corrected:** Bunny Hops ✓ when prompted with "what does 'how long' tell you?"
- Did not solve — Belle was tired and asked to stop.

## Observations

- Belle engaged well with the "superpowers" framing — responded positively to "you already know how to do this"
- Writing working down is becoming more natural, especially for Bunny Hops and Double Up
- Round & Fix arithmetic (the "fix" subtraction) needs monitoring — she understood the method but slipped on 525-3
- Method selection (knowing WHICH method to use) is emerging but not automatic yet — needed prompting to distinguish PM=Plus from Bunny Hops
- Fatigue set in after ~15 minutes — stopped on request (correct per study plan rules)
