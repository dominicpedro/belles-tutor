## Named Method: Bunny Hops

**Trigger phrase:** "Bunny Hops — draw the line, write your hops."
**Core rule:** A bunny always leaves footprints. DRAW the line. WRITE your hops.

**Written (test):**
```text
5:40 ---[20]---> 6:00 ---[1h]---> 7:00 ---[15]---> 7:15
                 20 + 1h + 15 = 1h 35min
```

**Typed (study sessions):**

```text
5:40 >[20m]=> 6:00 >[1h]=> 7:00 >[15m]=> 7:15 = 1h35m
```

Shorthand rules: `>[20m]=>` is one hop (mirrors the written arrows), `m` = minutes, `h` = hours, `=` only at end for total.
