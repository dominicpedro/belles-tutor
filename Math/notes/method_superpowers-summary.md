# Belle's 4 Superpowers

**Remember:** Working = marks. Even if you know the answer, write it down.

---

## PM = Plus

See "pm"? Plus 12. See a big number? Minus 12.

```text
2:30 pm > +12 > 14:30
```

---

## Bunny Hops

Any time question. Hop to nice numbers, write every hop, add them up.

A bunny always leaves footprints.

```text
6:45 >[15m]> 7:00 >[1h]> 8:00 >[20m]> 8:20 = 1h 35m
```

---

## Double Up

Big division. Start small, keep doubling until you reach the total.

```text
1x150 = 150
2x150 = 300
4x150 = 600
6x150 = 900 > 6
```

---

## Round & Fix

Ugly number near a round one? Round it up, do the easy sum, fix it.

```text
197 > round to 200 (added 3)
325 + 200 = 525
fix: 525 - 3 = 522
```
