# Round & Fix

**Use when:** Adding numbers that are close to a round number (like 198, 299, 4,95).

**Trigger:** "Round it up, do the easy sum, then fix it."

---

## Steps

1. ROUND the awkward number UP to the nearest 10/100/whole number
2. DO the easy addition
3. FIX IT -- take away whatever you added to round up

---

## On Paper (test)

```text
467 + 198 = ?

Round 198 up to 200 (added 2)
467 + 200 = 667
Fix: 667 - 2 = 665
```

## Typed (study sessions)

```text
467+198: +200=667, fix -2 => 665
```

---

## With money

```text
R34,50 + R9,95 = ?

Round R9,95 up to R10 (added 5c)
R34,50 + R10 = R44,50
Fix: R44,50 - R0,05 = R44,45
```

## Typed

```text
34.50+9.95: +10=44.50, fix -0.05 => 44.45
```
