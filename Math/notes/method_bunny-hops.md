# Bunny Hops

**Use when:** Any time question -- elapsed time, time difference, or find the start time.

**Trigger:** "Bunny Hops -- draw the line, write your hops."

**Rule:** A bunny always leaves footprints. If it's not written down, it didn't happen.

---

## Steps

1. DRAW the line (write start and end)
2. HOP to the next nice number (next whole minute or hour)
3. HOP the big chunk (full hours or minutes)
4. HOP the leftovers
5. ADD your hops

---

## On Paper (test)

```text
5:40 ---[20]---> 6:00 ---[1h]---> 7:00 ---[15]---> 7:15
                 20 + 1h + 15 = 1h 35min
```

## Typed (study sessions)

```text
5:40 >[20m]=> 6:00 >[1h]=> 7:00 >[15m]=> 7:15 = 1h35m
```

---

## "Find the start time" version

Don't go backwards. Flip the question:

"What time + duration = end time?"

Then Bunny Hop forward to check.
