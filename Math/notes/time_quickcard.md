# Time -- Quick Card

---

**Half past** = :30 | **Quarter past** = :15 | **Quarter to** = :45 (of the PREVIOUS hour)

---

### 24-Hour Time

| AM (morning) | Just write two digits | 7:20 am = **07:20** |
|---|---|---|
| **PM (afternoon/night)** | **Add 12** | 3:45 pm = **15:45** |
| Midnight | **00:00** | Noon | **12:00** |

**Belle's shortcut: PM = Plus!**
- See **pm**? → **Plus** 12 (to go TO 24-hour time)
- See a big number (13, 14, 15...)? → **Subtract** 12 (to go FROM 24-hour time)

**Going back? ALWAYS write AM or PM!**
Normal time uses the clock twice a day — 7:45 could be breakfast OR bedtime. Without AM/PM, no one knows which one you mean. Miss it on the test = lost mark.

---

### Elapsed Time (How long between two times?)

**Always draw a timeline. Jump in chunks:**

```
Start --[min to next hour]--> Full hour --[count hours]--> Last full hour --[leftover min]--> End
```

Add the chunks together. Done.

---

### Time Subtraction

**Borrow 60, not 10!**

| Need more seconds? | Borrow from minutes → get **60 seconds** |
|---|---|
| Need more minutes? | Borrow from hours → get **60 minutes** |

---

### Finding Start vs End Time

| Have start + duration? | **Add** → end time |
|---|---|
| Have end + duration? | **Subtract** → start time |

---

### Fractions of an Hour

| 1/2 = **30** min | 1/3 = **20** min | 1/4 = **15** min |
|---|---|---|
| 1/5 = **12** min | 1/6 = **10** min | 3/4 = **45** min |

---

### Three Rules

1. **60 not 10** — time borrows in 60s
2. **PM = +12** — always, every time
3. **Draw the timeline** — no shortcuts
