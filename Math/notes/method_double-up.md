# Double Up

**Use when:** Division word problems with big numbers. "How many can you buy?" or "How many fit?"

**Trigger:** "Find a small pair, then double up to the total."

---

## Steps

1. Find a SMALL pair you know (e.g. 2 x R350 = R700)
2. DOUBLE it (4 x R350 = R1 400)
3. Keep doubling or adding until you reach the total
4. Count how many you've got

---

## On Paper (test)

```text
R2 100 / R350 = ?

1 x 350 = 350
2 x 350 = 700
4 x 350 = 1 400
6 x 350 = 2 100  --> Answer: 6
```

## Typed (study sessions)

```text
1x350=350, 2x350=700, 4x350=1400, 6x350=2100 => 6
```
