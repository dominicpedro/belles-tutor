## Page 1 — Rounding Numbers (Number & Pattern)

### Concepts
- Rounding to the **nearest 10**: find which multiple of 10 is closest
- Rounding to the **nearest 100**: find which multiple of 100 is closest
- Rounding to the **nearest 5**: find which multiple of 5 is closest
- **Convention**: when a number is exactly halfway (e.g. 25, 350), round **UP**

### Key Rules
| Rounding to | Look at this digit | If 0–4 → round down | If 5–9 → round up |
|---|---|---|---|
| Nearest 10 | Ones digit | e.g. 34 → 30 | e.g. 38 → 40 |
| Nearest 100 | Tens digit | e.g. 230 → 200 | e.g. 254 → 300 |
| Nearest 5 | Ones digit | 0,1,2 → round down to 0/5 | 3,4 → round up to 5; 6,7 → round down to 5; 8,9 → round up to 0 |
