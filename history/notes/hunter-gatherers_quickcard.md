---

## Nomadic Life

- Moved for food and water
- Small groups, few belongings
- Shared everything → everyone equal
- Water stored in **ostrich egg shells** (sealed with grass/clay plug)

---

## Three Medicinal Plants

| Plant | Use |
|-------|-----|
| **Buchu** | Stomach aches, headaches, toothaches |
| **Hoodia** | Stops hunger + thirst (for hunters) |
| **Plant sap** | Snake bite treatment |

---

## Bow and Arrow

| Part | Made from |
|------|-----------|
| Bow | Elastic branch |
| String | Gemsbok back muscles |
| Arrow | Stone or bone |
| Poison | **Beetle** larvae (placed BEHIND the point) |

---

## The Hunt (10 steps)

Track → follow for days → shoot arrow → poison works slowly → follow until it falls → cut out arrow part → spears to finish → sharp stones to skin → bring meat to camp → **community feast**

---

## Rock Art

**Colours:** Red, yellow, white, black
**Paint binders:** Blood, egg white
**White paint:** White clay + water
**Tools:** Sticks (body), fingers (detail), hollow reed (spray)
**Meaning:** Religious symbols (NOT just everyday drawings)
**Archaeologist** = person who studies old objects

---

## Coat of Arms

**Motto:** !ke e: /xarra //ke
**Means:** "People who are different join together"
**Language:** San (honouring the first people of SA)

**Linton Panel:** Rock art from farm in Eastern Cape → now in SA Museum, Cape Town
**Two figures** greeting = unity + happiness

**Parts:** Sun, protea, knobkierie, shield, spear, San figures, elephant tusks, wheat, motto
