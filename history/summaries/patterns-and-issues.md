## Study Priority

### Key Vocabulary (all units)

- **ethnography** -- studying a group of people by observing how they live, their customs and daily life
- **hunter-gatherers** -- people who hunt animals and gather wild plants for food
- **nomadic** -- moving from place to place, not staying in one location
- **Stone Age** -- a period when people made tools from stone and bone
- **archaeology** -- studying the past by digging up and examining old objects and remains
- **stamina** -- the ability to keep going for a long time without getting tired
- **tracking** -- following animal footprints and signs to find them
- **prey** -- an animal that is hunted for food
- **poison** -- substance used on arrows to kill animals (from beetle larvae)
- **identity** -- who you are; what makes you or a group special
- **motto** -- a short sentence or phrase that represents what you believe in
- **coat of arms** -- an official symbol of a country
